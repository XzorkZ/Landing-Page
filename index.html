Welcome!

Lina rooms is a family run accommodation that offer “Hostel-ish” rooms for tourist, business travelers and hospital patient or their visitors. We are located in a quiet area, nearby Head hospital of Ljubljana. You can find supermarkets and bistro/restaurants 5 min walk away from us. The accommodation is nearby Art street Metelkova ( 5 min walk ) and Main bus/train station ( 13 min walk ). We are also within walking disctance from the city center and the old town. The accommodation offer 3 free parking space for its guests and a small shared kitchen for all visitors. Our rooms are simply decorated with all basic amenities you need for a good night sleep.
